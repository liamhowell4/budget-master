Getting started
1
Sign in
Go to budget-master-lh.web.app and sign in with Google, GitHub, or Apple. Your account is created automatically, no setup needed.
2
Set your budget caps (optional, but recommended)
Visit the Dashboard and set a monthly spending limit for each category, food, groceries, gas, and more. You'll get automatic in-chat alerts as you approach each limit.
3
Log your first expense
Open the Chat tab and type something like Coffee $5 this morning or Lunch $14 at Chipotle. The AI infers the category, date, and name automatically!
4
Ask questions about your spending
The chat isn't just for logging. Try How much have I spent on food this month? or What were my top 5 expenses last week?
A few other things you can do
Attach a receipt photo. Send an image of any receipt in the chat and the AI extracts the amount, merchant, and items automatically.
Edit or delete by asking. Made a mistake? Just say actually that was $6.50 or delete that last one and the AI handles it.
Set up recurring expenses. For rent, subscriptions, or any regular bill, just describe it once and the app will prompt you to confirm it each month automatically.
Voice on Apple Watch. If you install the iOS app, you can log expenses by speaking directly from your Apple Watch.
